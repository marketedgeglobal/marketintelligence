The Homeowner Assistance Fund, authorized by the American Rescue Plan Act, provides support to homeowners facing financial hardship associated with COVID-19. Through June 2024, HAF-funded programs have assisted over 549,000 homeowners, helping prevent mortgage delinquencies, defaults, foreclosures, and displacement.
